  **Last Updated:** April 10, 2025

  Thank you for using Tokoro! This Privacy Policy explains how Tokoro ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares

  **1. Information We Collect**

  We collect different types of information, including:

  - **Information You Provide Directly:**

  other information you choose to provide.
  - **Profile Information:** You may choose to add information to your profile, such as your interests, location, and

  upload to the Platform.

  - **Search Queries and Interactions:** We collect information about your searches, interactions with the AI-powered
  features, and the places you view and interact with on the Platform.

  - **Usage Data:** We collect information about how you use the Platform, such as the pages you visit, the features you
  use, the dates and times of your access, and the duration of your sessions.
  - **Device Information:** We may collect information about your device, including your IP address, browser type,
  operating system, device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  - **Location Data:** With your consent, we may collect your precise or approximate location data to provide
  location-based features and recommendations. You can typically control location sharing through your device settings.
  - **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** We use cookies, web beacons, and other similar technologies to collect
  information about your browsing activities and preferences. You can manage your cookie preferences through your
  browser settings.

  - **Information from Third Parties:**
  - We may receive information about you from third-party services if you connect your Tokoro account to those services
  (e.g., social media platforms). The information we receive depends on your privacy settings with the third-party
  service.

  **2. How We Use Your Information**

  We use your information for various purposes, including to:

  - **Provide and Maintain the Platform:** To operate the Platform, provide you with access to its features, and ensure
  its functionality.
  - **Personalize Your Experience:** To understand your preferences and interests, and to provide you with personalized
  recommendations, search results, and content based on our AI-powered features.
  - **Improve the Platform:** To analyze how users interact with the Platform, identify areas for improvement, and

  - **Communicate with You:** To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and send you important updates and
  notifications related to the Platform.
  - **Send Promotional Materials:** With your consent, we may send you newsletters, special offers, and other marketing
  communications about places and features that may interest you. You can opt-out of receiving these communications at
  any time.
  - **Ensure Security and Prevent Fraud:** To monitor and protect the security and integrity of the Platform, and to
  detect and prevent fraudulent activities.
  - **Comply with Legal Obligations:** To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  - **Analyze and Aggregate Data:** We may anonymize and aggregate your information with other users' information for
  statistical and analytical purposes.

  We may share your information with the following categories of recipients:

  - **Other Users:** If you post content publicly on the Platform (e.g., reviews, comments), this information will be

  the Platform, such as hosting providers, analytics providers, payment processors, and marketing service providers.

  development purposes.

  other third parties if required by law or legal process, or if we believe it is necessary to protect our rights,
  property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

  transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

  You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  - **Access and Correction:** You may access and update your account information through your profile settings. You can
  also request to access and correct any inaccurate personal information we hold about you.

  certain exceptions (e.g., legal obligations).
  - **Opt-Out of Marketing Communications:** You can opt-out of receiving marketing emails from us by following the
  unsubscribe instructions in those emails or by adjusting your notification settings.
  - **Location Data:** You can control the collection of your location data through your device settings.
  - **Cookies:** You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  - **Data Portability:** You may have the right to receive a copy of your personal information in a structured,
